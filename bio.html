No Project Too Large
No Detail Too Small

Where passion, professionalism and personality come together, there you’ll find Luisa Patterson, interior designer. As founder and creative director of LP Design Details, Luisa brings a breadth of knowledge gleaned from her experience working in the following areas:

  • Commercial
  • Residential
  • Hospitality
  • Retail Design

Whether it’s pulling together a big construction project from start to finish, or simply sitting down over a cup of coffee to help you choose paint colors, no project is too big or too small.

I’m perfectly comfortable working side by side with engineers, architects and industry partners to complete your large-scale design project, or being by your side as you choose the perfect entry table for your family home. Design is not only my profession, but my passion. I take incredible pride and immense joy in helping my clients realize their project’s design potential.

Education

As an interior designer, Luisa dedicated four years at the prestigious Design Institute of San Diego (DISD), earning her Bachelor’s of Fine Arts with an emphasis in interior design. During her coursework, she gained a deep knowledge of the history, future, and power of design in the following areas:

  • environmental studies
  • building codes
  • color theory
  • furniture design
  • history of fine arts
  • ergonomics
  • CAD (computer aided drafting)
  • construction documents
  • 3d software
  • space planning
  • business ethics
  • architectural history
  • textiles
  • ADA compliance

In addition to her intense coursework at DISD, Luisa was able to expand on her education with international studies in Italy, France and Mexico.

While I’ve been fortunate enough to experience the best of design in far off parts of the globe, I’m passionate about making design accessible for all. Every business and every home can benefit from the skills of a talented interior designer. Everyone deserves their best home possible.

Process & Philosophy

The interior design process may seem intimidating, but it doesn’t have to be. Luisa guides clients through a seamless, comfortable experience that’s as stress free as possible.

Any time you’re taking on a big construction project, or maybe even just repainting a room, there are a lot of steps involved. With so much to get done, and a budget to keep in mind, things can start to seem overwhelming. This is where my services are a life saver. I make the process fun and enjoyable--as it should be!

Luisa’s ability to deliver design projects on time and on (often under) budget with a smile on her face is what sets her apart.

I form a relationship with each client which allows me to develop a timeless, clean, organized and functional design which they’ll love for years to come. I consider every minute detail, all while keeping the big picture in mind--the forest and the trees.

Through Luisa’s broad network of industry partners, she’s often able to secure the best possible prices, without compromising any design details.

Design is my passion, my love. The look of amazement and happiness on my clients’ faces when they see their finished project for the first time is what keeps me going. This never gets old.
